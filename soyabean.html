Soybean

Legume

Soybean is a high-protein legume that enriches soil nitrogen. It is used for oil extraction, animal feed, and various food products like tofu and soy milk.

Season

Kharif

Water Requirement

450-700 mm

Soil Type

Well-drained loamy soil

Growth Duration

85-120 days

Expected Yield

2-3 tonnes/hectare

Common Diseases

  • Rust
  • Yellow Mosaic
  • Charcoal Rot
  • Pod Blight

Best Practices

  • 1. Treat seeds with Rhizobium culture
  • 2. Sow at 3-4 cm depth
  • 3. Maintain row spacing of 30-45 cm
  • 4. Avoid waterlogging
  • 5. Harvest when 95% pods turn brown
