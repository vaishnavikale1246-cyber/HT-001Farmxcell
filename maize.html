Maize

Cereal

Maize, also known as corn, is a versatile crop used for food, animal feed, and industrial purposes. It is rich in carbohydrates and provides essential vitamins.

Season

Kharif & Rabi

Water Requirement

500-800 mm

Soil Type

Well-drained sandy loam

Growth Duration

80-110 days

Expected Yield

5-8 tonnes/hectare

Common Diseases

  • Maize Dwarf Mosaic
  • Northern Leaf Blight
  • Stalk Rot
  • Ear Rot

Best Practices

  • 1. Plant at 5-7 cm depth with 60-75 cm row spacing
  • 2. Apply adequate nitrogen for high yields
  • 3. Irrigate at critical stages: knee-high, tasseling, grain filling
  • 4. Control weeds in first 40 days
  • 5. Harvest when moisture content is 20-25%
