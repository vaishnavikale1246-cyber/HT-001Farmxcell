Tomato

Vegetable

Tomatoes are one of the most popular vegetables grown worldwide. They are rich in vitamins A and C, and lycopene, making them highly nutritious.

Season

Year-round (with protection)

Water Requirement

400-600 mm

Soil Type

Sandy loam to clay loam

Growth Duration

60-90 days

Expected Yield

25-40 tonnes/hectare

Common Diseases

  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Bacterial Wilt
  • Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl

Best Practices

  • 1. Transplant seedlings 25-30 days old
  • 2. Maintain spacing of 60x45 cm
  • 3. Stake plants for support
  • 4. Apply mulch to conserve moisture
  • 5. Prune suckers for better yield
