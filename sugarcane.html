About Sugarcane

Sugarcane is a tropical perennial crop widely cultivated for sugar production. It is also used to produce jaggery, ethanol, and animal feed. India is one of the largest producers of sugarcane in the world.

Soil Requirement

Sugarcane grows best in deep, fertile, well-drained loamy soil. The soil should have good organic matter and proper irrigation facilities.

Water Requirement

It requires adequate irrigation, especially during dry periods. Drip irrigation is highly recommended for better yield and water conservation.
